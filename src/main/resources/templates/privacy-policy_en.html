Onul Privacy Policy

Onul Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") places great importance on protecting users' personal information and has established this Privacy Policy in compliance with global data protection laws, including the GDPR of the European Union, the CCPA of California, the APPI of Japan, and the Personal Data Protection Act of India.

1. Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information

The Company collects the following minimal personal information:

  • When signing up: Username, password, name, phone number, email address
  • During service use: Service usage records, access logs, IP information, device information, purchase history

2. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

The Company will destroy the personal information without delay once the purpose of collection and use has been achieved. However, information may be retained for a certain period if required by law.

3. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

The Company does not provide personal information to third parties without the user's consent, except as required by law.

4. Outsourcing and Overseas Transfer of Personal Information

To provide services, the Company may outsource some personal information processing to external companies or transfer it abroad. In such cases, appropriate protective measures will be taken in accordance with relevant laws.

5. Destruction of Personal Information

Personal information is safely destroyed after the purpose of collection and use has been achieved. Electronic files are deleted in a manner that cannot be recovered, and documents are shredded.

6. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal information at any time, and the Company will process these requests without delay.

7. Use of Cookies

The Company may use cookies to enhance the user experience, and users can refuse the use of cookies through their browser settings.

8. Data Protection Rights (Europe and Other Regions)

Users within the European Union have specific rights under the GDPR, and the Company complies with these regulations.

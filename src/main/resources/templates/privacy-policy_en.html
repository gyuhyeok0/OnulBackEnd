Onul Privacy Policy

Onul ("Onul") values the protection of users' personal information and has established this Privacy Policy to comply with global data protection laws, including the GDPR of the European Union, the CCPA of California, the APPI of Japan, and the PIPA (Personal Information Protection Act) of South Korea.

1. Purpose of Collecting and Using Personal Information

Onul collects only the minimum necessary personal information as follows:

  • During Sign-Up: User ID, password, name, phone number
  • After Sign-Up (Initial Information): Height, weight, gender, 1RM (for AI-based exercise analysis)
  • During Service Use: Service usage history, workout records, diet records
  • For Inquiries: Email address, inquiry details (for customer support)

Note: IP information, device information, and purchase history are managed by external service providers such as Firebase and RevenueCat.

2. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

Onul securely deletes personal information once the collection and use purpose is fulfilled. However, if retention is required by law, the information is stored securely for the legally mandated period before being deleted.

3. Collection and Use of Personal Information for Identity Verification

Onul collects the following personal information for identity verification in sign-up and password recovery services:

  • During Sign-Up: Phone number (for SMS verification)
  • During Password Recovery: Phone number (for SMS verification)

4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

Onul does not provide users' personal information to third parties. However, phone numbers are shared with external service providers such as AWS SNS for SMS verification.

5. Retention and Use Period of Identity Verification Data

Phone numbers collected for identity verification are securely deleted within 24 hours after verification.

6. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

Onul does not provide personal information to third parties without user consent. However, information may be provided in the following cases:

  • When legally required by relevant laws or legal procedures
  • When necessary for linking with essential external services (Firebase, RevenueCat, etc.)

7. Outsourcing and Overseas Transfer of Personal Information

For seamless service, Onul uses global cloud services (Firebase, RevenueCat, etc.), and some personal data may be processed overseas.

  • Transferred Data: Login information, service usage history, purchase records
  • Destination Countries: South Korea, USA, Europe, Japan, and other service regions
  • Protection Measures: Compliance with the privacy policies and data protection regulations of service providers (Firebase, RevenueCat, etc.)

8. Disposal of Personal Information

Onul securely deletes personal information after achieving the purpose of collection and use.

  • Electronic files: Permanently deleted using non-recoverable methods
  • Physical documents: Destroyed using a shredder

9. User Rights

Users may request access, modification, or deletion of their personal data at any time, and Onul will promptly process such requests.

  • Access and Modification: Requests can be made via the in-app “Contact Us” feature.
  • Deletion: Users can request deletion via “Settings” > “Contact Us” or “Withdraw Membership”. Legally required data will be retained, but all other personal data will be deleted immediately.

10. Data Protection Rights (EU, Japan, USA, South Korea, etc.)

  • Right to Access: Users can request a copy of their personal information held by Onul.
  • Right to Rectification: Users can correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: Users can request deletion of personal data under legal conditions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Users can request limited processing of their personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: Users can receive their personal data in a machine-readable format or transfer it to another service. (Applies to GDPR, CCPA regions)
  • Right to Object to Automated Decision-Making and Profiling: Users can demand explanations or refuse AI-based recommendations. (Applies to GDPR regions)

Users can exercise these rights via the in-app “Contact Us” feature, and Onul will process requests promptly in compliance with relevant laws.

11. Privacy and Advertising Policies

  • Onul uses third-party ad networks such as Google AdMob.
  • Google AdMob may collect advertising IDs for personalized ads and analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy for more details.
  • Onul does not directly use users' personal data for advertising.
  • Onul does not sell users' personal data to third parties.
  • Users can request personal data-related queries via the in-app “Contact Us” feature.
